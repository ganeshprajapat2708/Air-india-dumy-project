Our History

Air India is the flag carrier airline of India, headquartered in New Delhi. Founded by J. R. D. Tata as Tata Airlines in 1932, it was later nationalized in 1953. Today, it operates a fleet of Airbus and Boeing aircraft serving a variety of domestic and international destinations.
